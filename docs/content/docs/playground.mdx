Play hexagonal chess right here. Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted cell to move. The AI (heuristic MCTS, 500 simulations) responds automatically.

<Playground />

**Controls:**
- **New Game** — start a fresh game
- **Undo** — take back your last move (undoes both yours and the AI's response)
- **Flip Side** — switch to play as the other color
- **AI vs AI** — watch two AI players play against each other

The engine runs entirely in your browser via WebAssembly — no server needed. See the [Glinski rules](/docs/rules) page if you're new to hexagonal chess.
